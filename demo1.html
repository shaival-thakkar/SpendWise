How to Get Started

  • 1. Login or Sign Up: Create your account or log in to get started.
  • 2. Enter Your Credentials: Use your email and password. Never share bank or private details with any random site!
  • 3. Access Your Dashboard: View your budget, expenses, and virtual coins all in one place.
  • 4. Start Tracking: Enter your daily expenses or income with categories.
  • 5. Earn Rewards: Stay within your budget and collect virtual coins.
  • 6. Redeem & Grow: Use coins for financial rewards or invest in your future.

About SpendWise

SpendWise is your all-in-one solution to manage personal finances. We make it simple to set budgets, track daily expenses, and earn virtual coins for responsible spending. Whether you’re looking to save for a dream vacation or invest for the future, SpendWise has got you covered!
